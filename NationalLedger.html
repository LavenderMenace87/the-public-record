THE DEBT
WE DON'T PUBLISH

The United States government owes obligations it does not record in a single place, does not require any official to aggregate, and has no binding legal mechanism to address. The individual pieces are published — scattered across dozens of agency reports, on different schedules, with different methodologies, and no requirement that anyone add them up. This document does.

These figures are not secret. They come from CBO, SSA, NOAA, USGS, EPA, GAO, BIA, and the U.S. Treasury — official government sources. What does not exist is any law requiring them to be published together, any mechanism requiring Congress to respond when they grow, or any official bearing personal accountability for their accuracy in aggregate. The numbers are documented. The accountability is not.
